Our Story

Walkerbay Hotel Limited was established with a vision to create an oasis of tranquility and luxury on the shores of Lake Victoria. Located in the picturesque Muhuru-Bay, Migori County, our hotel serves as the perfect gateway to Tanzania and Uganda, offering unparalleled access to some of East Africa's most breathtaking destinations.

From our humble beginnings, we have grown to become one of the most trusted hospitality brands in the Lake Victoria region, known for our commitment to exceptional service, modern comfort, and authentic Kenyan hospitality.

Our property comprises 11 well-appointed Standard guest rooms and 2 luxurious suites, 2 restaurants, 2 bars, 24-hour In-Room Dining service, and a well-equipped gymnasium. With over 1500 sqm of meeting and banquet space, we are the perfect venue for seminars, large conferences, cocktail events, and corporate dinners.

Our Location

Strategically positioned at the heart of East Africa's most scenic region

Walkerbay Hotel offers uninterrupted views of Lake Victoria and is strategically located to provide easy access to key destinations in the region.

Our prime location in Muhuru-Bay places us at the gateway to Tanzania borders and the last end of Kenya bordering Uganda through Migingo Islands. We are surrounded by major tourist destinations including Mugabo Caves, Thimlich Ohinga and Masai Mara.

Key Location Advantages:

  • 45 minutes drive from Migori town
  • 10 minutes from Tanzania Border
  • 45 minutes from the Lichota Airstrip
  • Close to historical Mugabo Caves
  • Proximity to Thimlich Ohinga archaeological site
  • Gateway to Masai Mara National Reserve
  • Access to Lake Victoria fishing and boating
  • Stunning views of Africa's largest lake

Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, our location provides the perfect base to explore the rich cultural heritage and natural beauty of the Lake Victoria region.

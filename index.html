About Walkerbay Hotel

Discover the perfect blend of comfort, nature, and adventure at our luxurious lakeside resort

Walkerbay Hotel comprises of 11 well-appointed Standard guest rooms and 2 suites, 2 restaurants, 2 bars, 24-hour In-Room Dining service, and a well-equipped gymnasium. Walkerbay Hotel provides the perfect venue for meetings and events with more than 1500 sqm of meeting and banquet space, perfect for seminars, large conferences, cocktail events and corporate dinners.

The property offers uninterrupted views of Lake Victoria, The Walkerbay Hotel is located only 45 minutes drive from Migori town, 10 minutes from Tanzania Border and 45 minutes from the Lichota Airstrip, the historical Mugabo Caves, Thimlich Ohinga.

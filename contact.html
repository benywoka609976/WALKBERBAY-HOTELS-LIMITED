Our Location

Strategically positioned at the gateway to Tanzania and Uganda with stunning lake views

How to Find Us

Walkerbay Hotel is conveniently located in Muhuru-Bay, Migori County, offering easy access to key destinations in the region.

Location Advantages:

  • 45 minutes drive from Migori town
  • 10 minutes from Tanzania Border
  • 45 minutes from the Lichota Airstrip
  • Close to historical Mugabo Caves
  • Proximity to Thimlich Ohinga archaeological site
  • Gateway to Masai Mara National Reserve
  • Access to Lake Victoria fishing and boating
  • Stunning views of Africa's largest lake

Transportation Options:

  • Airport transfers available
  • Car rental services
  • Secure parking facilities
  • Local taxi services
  • Boat transfers to nearby islands

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our hotel and services

What is your check-in and check-out time?

Check-in time is from 2:00 PM, and check-out time is by 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability and additional charges.

Do you offer airport transfers?

Yes, we offer airport transfer services from Lichota Airstrip (45 minutes away) and other nearby airports. Please contact our reservations team in advance to arrange your transfer.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash (Kenyan Shillings, US Dollars), credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard), and M-Pesa payments. For M-Pesa payments, use Paybill number 123456 with your booking ID as the account number.

Is Wi-Fi available throughout the hotel?

Yes, we offer complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi throughout the hotel, including all guest rooms, restaurants, and public areas.

Do you have facilities for guests with disabilities?

Yes, we have accessible rooms and facilities for guests with disabilities. Please inform us of any specific requirements when making your reservation.

Can I host events or conferences at your hotel?

Absolutely! We have comprehensive conference facilities that can accommodate events from 10 to 200 people. Contact our events team for customized packages and availability.

Do you offer special rates for long stays or group bookings?

Yes, we offer special rates for extended stays and group bookings. Please contact our reservations team for customized quotes based on your specific requirements.
